Coming in next, we have the Brussels Griffon. A tiny, non-shedding dog that you probably saw on
TV. And not just because of their looks. The Brussels Griffon breed is extremely trainable,
and are so loyal they will most likely follow you around all day. The breed doesn't require
much exercise at all, but will require quite a bit of cuddles and affection. That sounds like a
win-win. The breed is also non-shedding and hypoallergenic, so no worries about cleaning up
tons of hair from your movie star dog.
