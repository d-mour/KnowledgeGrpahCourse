The British Shorthair is one of the most popular cat breeds in the world, known for its calm and easygoing temperament. The breed has a dense, plush coat with a thick undercoat that sheds moderately throughout the year. British Shorthairs are low-maintenance when it comes to grooming — brushing the coat once a week is enough to remove loose hair and keep it looking neat. This breed adapts well to apartment living and gets along easily with children and other animals. British Shorthairs are known for their robust health and typically live between 12 and 17 years.
