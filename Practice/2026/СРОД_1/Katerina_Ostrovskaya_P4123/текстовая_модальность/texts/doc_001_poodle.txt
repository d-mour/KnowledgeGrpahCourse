The Poodle is one of the most popular hypoallergenic dog breeds in the world. It has a thick, curly coat that does not shed, which means less dander and hair in the environment. Because dander is the main carrier of the Can f1 protein — the primary allergen in dogs — Poodles are considered a safe choice for people with allergies. Owners with asthma or allergic reactions often report that living with a Poodle causes little to no symptoms. Regular grooming is still recommended to keep the coat clean and to further reduce allergen buildup.
