The Siberian is a large, semi-longhaired cat breed originally from Russia. Despite its thick and dense coat, the Siberian is often listed among hypoallergenic cat breeds. Studies suggest that Siberian cats produce lower levels of the Fel d1 protein, which is the primary allergen found in cat saliva, skin, and fur. Because Fel d1 is spread through grooming and shedding, lower protein levels mean reduced allergic reactions for sensitive individuals. Many allergy sufferers report being able to live comfortably with a Siberian cat without significant symptoms. However, individual reactions can vary, and spending time with the cat before adoption is always recommended.
